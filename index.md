# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 7, 2026**

Sonder ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## TL;DR

Sonder stores all of your data **locally on your device**. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information. There are no servers, no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Sonder stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- Therapy session notes, insights, and reflections
- Mood entries and feelings
- Personal goals, habits, and tasks
- Therapy schedule and appointment dates
- App preferences (theme, notifications, etc.)
- Your name (if you choose to provide it)

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to us or any third party.

## How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in an encrypted local database on your device using Apple's standard secure storage. If you enable App Lock, your data is additionally protected by Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode.

## Third-Party Services

Sonder does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. We do not integrate with Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any similar service.

## Notifications

Sonder uses local notifications (delivered by your device) to remind you of therapy appointments and journaling moments. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

## Permissions

Sonder may request the following permissions:

- **Notifications** — to deliver session reminders. You can enable or disable this anytime.
- **Face ID / Touch ID** — only if you enable App Lock. Used to unlock the app.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone — because we never have access to it in the first place.

## Data Export

You can export your sessions to a PDF at any time using the "Export to PDF" feature in Settings. The PDF is generated on your device. Where you choose to share or save it is entirely up to you.

## Data Deletion

You can delete individual entries at any time. To delete all of your data, uninstall the app from your device.

## Children's Privacy

Sonder is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be available within the app and at this URL. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
